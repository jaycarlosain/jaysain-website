About

Jay Sain

Jay Sain is a Puerto Rican-Indian filmmaker, photographer, musician, and artist based in Brooklyn, New York.

His creative practice focuses on transforming everyday scenes into surreal visual spaces. Stark architectural elements, natural illumination, and human figures combine in his work, where people become secondary to their environments. Abstract qualities throughout his imagery create an inherent sense of mystery.
